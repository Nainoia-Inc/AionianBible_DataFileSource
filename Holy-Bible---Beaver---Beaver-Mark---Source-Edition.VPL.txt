MAR 1:1 Hatseto mawotich oochu Jesus Christ, Tikeaootli ma chue;
MAR 1:2 Takeh nichatinkles akehe koonachatya kehe, Oosooasi sakewotihche nachatyea atghuni oochu naaootle.
MAR 1:3 Hligi otyesta tike kemweh toowe ooli, Atghuni oochu ahasoonesun, aghahati tsiwoeseh kwetooyakehe.
MAR 1:4 John tane tukeghingklit kemweh, hatse tane tatilon tunaghakeooskliti ahtilon otlwechi keochehlalon kehe aghametseli oneochetolel asilon.
MAR 1:5 Kahchu yinkhadeshalon tike okehe otsi Judaea, kahchu aghatane yateh otsi Jerusalem, kuihe sahke Jordan aghanetye tukukeghinklilon, koometselihi kooatuoochatihi.
MAR 1:6 John ghatai manatsunne ghigheze gha eghayulon natun atyetlalon atazi syeh kahchu atazi syeh ihe nanihklulon: oohchu yaskesi yu achalinoo yu ayilai yetsetlon;
MAR 1:7 Oochu toowe ooli yehtilon, yetsi ootelon, Hligi sooniya sataihchi anati, nakyeklule aghoo atu enasoo machi chunoostyeli mamakeoosut.
MAR 1:8 Suni la tu ihe naghakyeihklit: ahwole atai tu ihe naghakyeihlili Atsinde Oochu ihe.
MAR 1:9 Occhu gheise wazeluh ayi dzine, Jesus otsidesha Ion Nazareth Galilee otsi, John tuyakeghinklilon Jesus Jordan otsi.
MAR 1:10 Kwete tu tsi tghatyeza tsiwohzon, wailon yake kwa matahatsataghiolon, yakeotine yetsi natezalon oietli etye:
MAR 1:11 Oochu otech yake otsi atitsuklon, toowela ooli oti ihe, Nila otaihchi nwasti Sachue.
MAR 1:12 Kwete atsinde achu tike tsi yatehalon kemwehchi.
MAR 1:13 Koochi kemwehchi atu tane nuklechi tioketi dzineke kooali, yakaotehun Metseli aghaghatai; kahchu ghatai metseli ihkyateslon; kahchu oochu woei oochu yetsinatilon.
MAR 1:14 Kootoo John kwechaghinhtyilon tane kwechaghinhtyinti, Jesus yuhchidesha Galilee, koohootyeh otech oochu Nagha Tgha makaoonadzutti otsi,
MAR 1:15 Kahchu toowela ooli, Tyeneti Nagha Tgha kyeyi: naghametselihi kyechahli, oochu otech oochu aha kootahti.
MAR 1:16 Ihtyezon tu chok Galilee tghamweh klontyesha, yailon Simon yu Andrew yu mekye yu tyemil tayesklu tu chok: atai tane taesklu tane eli.
MAR 1:17 Oochu Jesus toowela ooli yehti, Sakyetahtyel, kooh hanahooslihi oohchu hluge kehchi enahooslihi tane niyutooleli chon otsi.
MAR 1:18 Kwete taghatyemile echaghanelelon kikyehtyeshalon.
MAR 1:19 Oohchu oontsutli tontyo tyesha, yaih James Zebedee machue, kahchu John mekye, atilon tachin ela ehkye atyi tyemil keyatinklulon.
MAR 1:20 Kwete tyechi yehtilon: kwa taghata echughanelilon tachin ela ehkye atyi Zebedee oochu keotihche yu, oohchu kikyetyesklit.
MAR 1:21 Oohchu kochi ghatyestyetl Capernaum; kahchu kwete taneghaotatyech kwa kwiya menejitti dzineneh tane chehotyeh.
MAR 1:22 Oneghanintyellon yaotihche: kooatinti yaotatiheh otech oochu tatihi natsuti kehchi ayihoo mawoochuli, atu adeskles tane kehchi.
MAR 1:23 Oohchu iehti kweh atiih hligi teze ta kwa taneghaotatyech yaghati metseli ayiih; kahchu ootyestalon,
MAR 1:24 Toowe ooli, Atu aghaititaniah; atu ooli nagha assitli iyo aoohti, nuni Jesus Nazareth otsi? Yuhchitiyala aghayaonila? Atanasti nelesi Nagha Tgha Maatsinde Oochu ooih.
MAR 1:25 Jesus toowela ooli yehti, yachioote, Atu oonatih yehti, setseghatiyah yetsun tsi.
MAR 1:26 Kootoo aghaghatai tsunale too yayaghinchil, oohtye ootyestalo, yetsi hadesha.
MAR 1:27 Aghanetye ooghatesta, aghanetye chu koohtaghoati, tiyeohtye, yetike aloontye? ye tane tsi oochatihi koonte? Aha kootesti otech oochu gha saghatai mayis atu natset.
MAR 1:28 Koohchu oonthe atyeh meghoyuh yito kwa Galilee.
MAR 1:29 Ayite otsi, ghaghatestyetlon taneghaotatyechi kwa otsi, kweghaghintyetl Simon yu Andrew yu kwa, James chu John chu.
MAR 1:30 Simon tsiyue ma tyelasatyi teskoone ayila, oohchu koohoohati.
MAR 1:31 Yechidesha yila inchut, kahchu tahyatinti; kwete ayi teskoone ayila yecheli, ayi oota yakeotihch.
MAR 1:32 Hatse kahkachi, naghioo, kiya nanila yatitine, yaghi chu mazichi yaghatai metselihi.
MAR 1:33 Kahchu atghe ehlaititi tane natihti tyechiklaghatati tusklahti.
MAR 1:34 Kahchu atghazi tatihi akooyilalon ayeeah natlone yehtanintyelon, kooyaghatai metselihi natlon oonteslalon; kahchu atu ketselihoo wahti iyao-ohti, achi atayatilon ayiwooh ayehti.
MAR 1:35 Kahchu hatlindo, setsehto tsitike satalon, hadesha, kahchu koondeshalon aghasuchalechi, kahchu iehti yachutatyeheh.
MAR 1:36 Kahchu Simon yehkyatehchi kikyehtestyetllon.
MAR 1:37 Kikihtitsi, toowela ooli kiyehtilon, Atghe tane ninkhaghanatgha.
MAR 1:38 Towehchu yehti, Tane natehtsi otsidooshai kooche oohoostihi oochu, kootatooshigha lasasta aoontye.
MAR 1:39 Kahchu tane tsiwoghintalon taneghaotatyech kwa kwe Galilee, kahchu atsinde metseli ghayatehalon.
MAR 1:40 Kahchu machitatestyel tane tsusachi yaghoone yatsi nioonunisit, kahchu machi nachustachaklih, kahchu toowela ooli yehtilon Kooh koodinti ate oochu asaghoonlehassi.
MAR 1:41 Kahchu Jesus, atyetastihtoo madzye tatilon nati gha tiesunni ihe, yetsi tahtatitichlon, kahchu yakatehtich lon, kahchu toowela ooli yehtilon, Kooh koosti; kwakoola oochu anatyeh.
MAR 1:42 Tsiwohzon otatyech otye, susuchi yetselilon, kahchu oochu oohtyelon.
MAR 1:43 Kahchu yatatilon tsiwohzon itintiah, kahchu oochu tyehalon;
MAR 1:44 Kahchu toowela ooli yehtilon, Atu kahchu ooli ataoonti niwotine: kahchu achutiya, nazi yueh taneghaotatyech metihi, kahchu khaniah naoochu oohtyi ihe yaghi Moses za kyehchi, ata kiyutihi.
MAR 1:45 Kahchu hadesha, oohtye awonetye khootawootyelon, kahchu tike okehe khootawotyelon, kwa hlilon Jesus atu tanetghakweya tyetachatlihi kwa iyu meheaoontye, ahwole kemwehchi tike alilon, kahchu kiyinkhatestyetllon ewonetyetsi otsi.
MAR 2:1 Kahchu kwiyalon Capernaum tahke dzine oolitatatich wazilate; kahchu kooachatilon ayiti nito kwa oota ko-ochati ihe mewoyu.
MAR 2:2 Kwete natlone tyechi ghlaghatilon, kwa hlilon taghadesmanlon atulitah ookuzze ooli ghintlon kye, kwa hlilon tusla aghoo ooghata taghadesman ghintlon kye; kahchu sa yatatilon.
MAR 2:3 Kahchu kiyinkhatestyetl yatilonooh yatitihi ihe, tyentane kooyahtyel.
MAR 2:4 Kahchu atu ugha otsighatatyel iyua aoontye ootaneghutl kye, nighatilalon anetiyi ootaneghutl kye alinti; kootoo ghaghatagheoo yatilone, kitaghintsut takeustyi tyela satyechti.
MAR 2:5 Kootoo Jesus wai aha oochati, toowela ooli yehtilon ayi yatilone, Sechue, nametselihi naoonchatuleli.
MAR 2:6 Ahwole tahkine ayiti tyechii adesklesne, kahchu koodzye otsi indighateshalon,
MAR 2:7 Yeoogha ti teze atu oochu ateotye? mea metsehhi ka oontoolelassi kootahtye Nagha Tgha zon?
MAR 2:8 Kwete Jesus ataotio yaghatei intidesakehe, toowela ooli yehtilon, Yehoo oohtye naghadzye ihe intidaa aoontye?
MAR 2:9 Tyeyi meotena achatooti kehe yatilone, Nametselihi naoonchatoolel; koolehe, Tsitike sinta, nustyi neodiah?
MAR 2:10 Atawahtihi iyu aoontye hligi teze Machue natsutti ayehi chode tike kakametselihi naghaoontooleli, (toowela ooli yehtilon yatilone,)
MAR 2:11 Toowela nesiih, Niiya, nustye nioodiah, nakwa otsidiya.
MAR 2:12 Kwa kwete niiya, ustyi nioodioo, kahchu hadesha aitye yai; kwa hlilon atghe yaooli, kiyaooli Nagha Tgha, toowela ghatyeh, Atu haoontyedi oohwetyi tahooli.
MAR 2:13 Kahchu tu chok meootyesha; atghe aghanetye machichatyesha, kwa yaotatich.
MAR 2:14 Intyizon ghayalu, yailon Levi Alphaeus ma chue sata sooniya mechetilichti, toowela ooli yehtilon, Sakyetiya. Kahchu niiya yekehtesha.
MAR 2:15 Hesai wazeluh Jesus yakwa kwesata atsitsoo, natlone tanechowhoine chu metseline chu Jesus yakahtehtsi yaotatichne chu: oohchu ghintlone, kooketestyetl chu.
MAR 2:16 Kootoo hatseto adesklesne chu Phariseene chu kiyaichi inka aghatsitsoo tanechowhoine chu metseline chu, toowela ooli yehtilon yaotatichne, Yehoo yakaatsits aoontye yakah chu tuaton tane chowhoine chu metseline chu?
MAR 2:17 Kootoo Jesus ootitsahoo, toowela ooli yehti, Yaghine oochu oontyene atu kiyinkhasootati yutane, yatitihizon: atu yuhchideya tyechi kooyusine tyeswe yaghitane, ahwole ayi atu tyeswe yaghitane chodeh kyetsahanohoolehe koometselihi ooghatihuali.
MAR 2:18 Kahchu yaotatichne John chu Phariseene chu ooneaghatatihya toonkye: kahchu yuhchihatestyetl toowela ooli, yehoogha yaotatichne John chu Phariseene chu ooneatatehya aoontye, ahwole nuni maghotintichne atu ooneatatehya?
MAR 2:19 Kahchu Jesus toowela ooli yehti, Maskyege la kwa nichiyehi kooyehkyatechi ooneatatulela la? kooyehkyatechton atu ooneatatehya iyu aoontye.
MAR 2:20 Ahwole kwa kwete khoonahwozetasi, kootoo mka tuchainklitne kwiya kwa oochayihate, kahchu ayi dzine ooneatatuyelassi.
MAR 2:21 Atu chu ooli koonde ale ooli tsatse ihe kyechatoohkatassi, yaikoole koonde ihe kechaghinghati kwa hlilon hleooyu awotiassi.
MAR 2:22 Kahchu atuline tata ahklitassi chiche tu koonde satsoone tontyeto otsi: atu chu chiche tu ooli tsatse tataoohklit ootookyezasi, kahchu natesklitchiche tu, kahchu mataootsayih oontookyezasi: ahwole chiche tu koonde ooli ta koonde chuhklitassi.
MAR 2:23 Kahchu otles tghah niya achuniyehti Menejitti Dzine; kahchu yaotichne makye ghatehlu atghe inchaghakinesla otles.
MAR 2:24 Kahchu Phariseene toowehchu vehti, Nye, yehoo koohghaghati aoontye Menejitti Dzine atu tu dzine kooh ghatsati iyu aoontye?
MAR 2:25 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti, Atu makyeh ootahtyech tah oolila David aoontli kootoo intaklinto kahchu tapatoo, atane chu ayi vehkyatiche chu?
MAR 2:26 Kwiye sikooh Nagha Tgha kwa, intyizon Abiathar taneghaotatyechi metihi chok acha, kahchu kiyatsetslon otles oochu atuline yetsitslon gha yai zon taneghaotatyech, kahchu yayigholon ayi yehkyatiche.
MAR 2:27 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti, Menejitti Dzine achilalon tane gha, kahchu atu chu tane Menejitti Dzine gha:
MAR 2:28 Ayigho teze Chue ghakaoodzut Menejitti Dzine.
MAR 3:1 Kahchu tyetachatlihi kwa kwiya: ayiti teze natyelon mila taneskyelon.
MAR 3:2 Kahchu kiyunailon, ooka ooli asityeh kha Menejitti Dzine nai; kitalih iyu aoontye.
MAR 3:3 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti ayi teze mila taneskye, Hatelon atichi nazutooh.
MAR 3:4 Tooweh chu yehti, Oochu la oochu keoochuahch Menejitti Dzine, ketseliooh keoochuahch? tane ghaghatai gha kasoochatooti gha koole? Ahwole atu ooghati.
MAR 3:5 Kahchu anetye klokanatghalon madzyehatsutooh, kyeoocheklah ihe tane dzye natsutti, tooweh chu yehti teze, Tahtatinti. Ooh kahchu tahtatintieh: oohchu mila natsaghinhtalon achune kehchi.
MAR 3:6 Kahchu Phariseene achughatestyelon, kwete hla nighohanintalon yai chu Herodianuk, tihakehe soo ookitoohtelu.
MAR 3:7 Ahwole Jesus achutyezha yaghotine chu tu chok tsi: Kahchu tane natlone Galilee otsi yu Judaea otsi yu yekehtestyetl.
MAR 3:8 Kahchu Jerusalem otsi, kahchu Idumaea otsi, kahchu o-one otehchi otsi Jordan, kahchu aghatane ahthaze Tyre yu Sidon yu, ghintlon ke, ooghatitsuk chi ayi oochu keoahchi, kiyinkhadesha.
MAR 3:9 Kahchu yaotatichne tatilon, ela ckok natsutli ootagha iiti wothoogha kooghohtyi tane natlon ke kha ati makechichiticha.
MAR 3:10 Atane natlone yaghihti ihe; kwa hlilon yinkghanotyesuts yakatutihikha, anetye ayi tatihi akooyahine.
MAR 3:11 Tane yaghatai mindicheli kahchu kiyainchi ate, kyechinachustaklih, kahchu ghatyezul toowe la ooli kiyehti ihe. Nuni Nagha Tgha Chue nelaoontye.
MAR 3:12 Kooh oochu atu soohchiwoti atu atachutihikha.
MAR 3:13 Kahchu khis ehkyeghiyalon, kahchu ayizon ayuti tyechi yehti: yachidesha.
MAR 3:14 Kahchu keneti onketi matgha kliniyanila, yekyeootichigha, kahchu achutehalon udzih oohootigha.
MAR 3:15 Kahchu ayulhi gha natsutti, kahchu atsinde metselihi hayatuhi gha:
MAR 3:16 Kahchu Simon yindizi yakechioosun Peterlon;
MAR 3:17 Kahchu James Zebedee machue, kahchu John, James mekye; kahchu nindizi yakechioosun Boanerges, ayi nahthunni uskyege chatyeh:
MAR 3:18 Kahchu Andrew, kahchu Philip, kahchu Bartholomew, kahchu Matthew, kahchu Thomas, kahchu James Alphaeus machue, kahchu Thaddaeus, kahchu Simon ayi Canaanite,
MAR 3:19 Kahchu Judas Iscariot, mazi atahootalon: kahchu nito kwa kwighaghintyetl.
MAR 3:20 Kahchu natlone natsughanestlalon, ehtye kwa hlilon atu otles atsitslon natlonke.
MAR 3:21 Kootoo meootinoo ootitsukoo, achughatestyetl kiyuhchutigha: aghatune kooghatyeh, Tinditaodesooissi.
MAR 3:22 Kahchu adesklesne yuhchighatestyetl Jerusalem otsi ghati, Ayehi Beelzebub, kahchu yaghatai metselihi iiti otsi hayateha.
MAR 3:23 Kahchu koohchu yinkhawoti, kahchu too we yehti, tyecha otsi aghaghatai metselihi hatsatoohtyel oontye ayi aghaghatai metselihi?
MAR 3:24 Kahchu hlahti tane natehti intsake yawoinklit ate, ayiti tike ii oowotoehassi.
MAR 3:25 Kahchu ayi nito kwa yaooghinkliti ate, ayi nito kwa oowotoehassi.
MAR 3:26 Kahchu aghaghatai metselihi ataniiya ate, kahchu tezi yanile ate, atu oowotoehassi.
MAR 3:27 Atuline kweyali iyua aoontye teze natsutti kwa, yenatunne yatatulelu, hatse tahyaghesklu teze natsutti; atezon ya kwa yaghonile iyu aoontye.
MAR 3:28 Kwa hli anaghaisilon, Atghe metselihi kooya oochatoolelassi tane uskyege, kahchu soocha oochatihi:
MAR 3:29 Ahwole ayi ketselioo atii Menejitti Oochu atu ihlaotateoo ayehi kha, ahwole seghaai ayehi nioonizutti oonchatalihi:
MAR 3:30 Ayighoo ayehti, Ayehi aghaghatai metselihi.
MAR 3:31 Kahchu mekye chu ma chu iiti otsighatestyetl, kahchu tsehtsi nazutooh, kilati, kiyinkhaooti.
MAR 3:32 Kahchu tane natlone yaka tghanatihtsi, tooweh chu kiyehti, Nea, noo chu nehtghizinne chu tsehtsi naghatehya ninkhaghanata.
MAR 3:33 Kahchu ateyatetla, toowehchu yehti, Mea anne eli, setghezinne koole?
MAR 3:34 Kahchu khlonkanatgha ayi yaka khlontyetsi, kahchu toowela yehti, Ahi anne chu sehtghezoon chu!
MAR 3:35 Atunne zon kooh ghawoteassi ayi Nagha Tgha indi, ayi akye chu, ustye chu, unne chu.
MAR 4:1 Kahchu tyigha tane ootatech tu chok tghamweh: kahchu natlone yinkhadesha, kahchu tachin ela ehkyiya, kahchu tu chok tih ali; kahchu atghe tane natlone tike wohzon.
MAR 4:2 Kahchu yachioteh ooli natlon yughotyeh, kahchu toowe ehti otsiootihi,
MAR 4:3 Oochu atahtsuk; Oochu hadesha otles alihi tane anihyihi niyilihi gha:
MAR 4:4 Kahchu intyizon achunihyehti, tahkine yainkhyi tinkuzze, kahchu chua etye yaghatehtyetllon.
MAR 4:5 Kahchu tahke nooghinklit tsyetgha, atu oonchadi tike ooliite; kahchu kwete ghanaghiyu, atu tike tatghoe:
MAR 4:6 Ahwole chelon sa niniya, maziche ninihklitlon; aha oona maghaye toontooeyi, ayi ihe taihtsutii.
MAR 4:7 Kahchu tahke nooghinklit ghoos chine chok tgha, kahchu ghoos chine chok ghanaghiyu, kahchu klighatanityetUon, kahchu atuli machiche oolilon.
MAR 4:8 Kahchu achu nooghinklitlon tike oochu ti, kahchu machiche ooghilyelon ghanaghiyu yu kahchu tainyezho; kahchu yahlonihyu tahke taoketyi tahke chu edzetaiti ketyi, tahke chu keoneti ketyi.
MAR 4:9 Koohchu yehti, Yaghi matsaghe ooli, inkai ootootsuke yehti.
MAR 4:10 Kootoo chlatyo ayi yehkatyelichi yaghi chu keneti onketi matgha tawotya yehti kawotzutne gha.
MAR 4:11 Kalichu toewela ooli yehti, Naghunni aghaoochuneoh aoontye atawohtihiglia tyewotye hai koosti Nagha Tgha kyeyi: ahwole ayine tsehtsi natihne, atghe ooli keowaahch yehe achatih:
MAR 4:12 Wai oochui, kahchu atu hlyenahotihi gha; kahchu ooghatitsukooh ooghatootsukassi lon, kahchu atu klaghatootsukassilon; koohchu oolita ghooghanetyela, koometselihi kooya oone oochatulel akhe.
MAR 4:13 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti, Tiyeghakehe atawahtiha ooli kehe yaghaghahtai kehe? koohchu yeghateh atawahtiha atghe otech?
MAR 4:14 Oonehyene oonehyoh sa.
MAR 4:15 Kahchu aghatane tinkuzze tatihne sa chunihyehti; ahwole kootoo ooghatitsukassi kooh ghaghatai metseli kwoto otsidesha, kahchu yazake yuhniyatiooh yadzye ta otsi.
MAR 4:16 Kahchu aghatane chunihye ine tsye tgha; kootoo sa ooghatitsuk, kwete neooatiooh soochanetye;
MAR 4:17 Kahchu atu ghai ighutaklilon aghatane hahte, kwa tiootsi zon ghaghatalon, ootlwe kootoo sookhaootaya kootoo hlachatatane niwoya sa tachi kwete chahte intighatesah.
MAR 4:18 Kahchu aghatane ayi yachunihyene ghoos chok chine tgha; ayi sa ooghatitsuh,
MAR 4:19 Kooh tidi tike indi, kahchu mihe atsunnetahe yu mihe tatintlon yu, kahchu machachintye achu ooli kwenachaghila otcho ooli sa, kahchu atu machiche ooli.
MAR 4:20 Kahchu ayi mehoochunihyehe tike oochute; ayi ootitsukne sa, kahchu niooatioone, kahchu machiche ooli, tahke taoketyi enetyi, tahke chu edzetaitiketyi, tahke chu keonetiketyi.
MAR 4:21 Kahchu toowela yehti, Nachila takooche nichualli ghotwetghulle yue take ustyi koole? koohchu atu nichiooite takoochechine?
MAR 4:22 Agha atuli nachunehi atu mewotuti; kahchu atuli tanetcha maghuchata iyu aoontye, ahwole atachuteassi.
MAR 4:23 Koole matsaghe oolyetane inkai ootootsukassi.
MAR 4:24 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti, Tyeke ayi ootahtsukki: ayi ihe tikeghaklyeli, ayieh nagha tsi tiketsalelassi: kahchu naghai ootaghahtsukki, ootehchi nahachuallassi.
MAR 4:25 Yaghi ayehi, machuallassi: kahchu yaghi atu ayehi, mehwayachutallassi koole ayi ayehi.
MAR 4:26 Kahchu toowela ooli yehti, Koohia ootye oontye Nagha Tgha kyeyi, hletye teze oochunehyehti oodihyitl keoontye;
MAR 4:27 Kahchu satyi, kahchu niiya hatleke chu dzine chu, kahchu achunehyehti ghanaghihu, atu ataoti noontye sikooh.
MAR 4:28 Ghoo yaghi tike atai hahte otsi ghanaghihu; hatse kloke eli, kahchu hachaghinaghiyu, kahchu otlweh ootles.
MAR 4:29 Kahchu yaghi chiche ghanaghihuta, kwete atataz, khoo chotazze dzine eli.
MAR 4:30 Towehchu yehtilon, Tyechi otsi gha Nagha Tgha kehchi niwowotal aoontye manatake? koole hletyi indi nighaootal aoontye?
MAR 4:31 Hleoontye oochuniliyehti ehlatye yutanikluchi machiche, kwa nichalesta oonahyehe tike, eyi zon natsutli anetye oochu ihyehe tike tih:
MAR 4:32 Kwa aochelesta, oochu nichulesta ayi zon otehchi yaincha anetye nichulihchassi, kahchu matatse natlon; kahchu ayiti chua yayue sacha iyue tehtsi.
MAR 4:33 Kahchu ayi etye akyehchi oochatihi ihe ehyatatilon, ghanawesittih oohasoohtsoo gha.
MAR 4:34 Ahwole atu ya-i-ti-tyes ate atu yachiwotyehlon: kahchu sahta aghatinta, yaotatichne atghe yatatilon.

MAR 8:3 Achu kootyehaitye tagha kwa tsi yaghatipatooh, woianh oohatootsitassi, natlone tontye tsi otsighatelyu.
MAR 8:4 Kahchu yaotatichne atetyataih, Tyechi otsi gha tayanooto tine tane otlestyes ihe chode kemweh?
MAR 8:5 Kahchu Taitya otles tyes ahi yehtilon? kahchu toowe ghati, Taiuchi.
MAR 8:6 Tane tsi tsiwohzon tahtsi tike wozon: ayi ninayatila taiuchi otles tyes, kahchu merci ehti, kahchu yataghiyits, kahchu yaotatichne ghila oochu yaniyuleli; kwa kiyaniyila ine tane gha.

MAR 8:9 Kahchu ine eghaghinta, oolita tiko keoneti keoneti ketyi enetyi silon: kahchu achudehalon.

MAR 8:11 Kahchu Phariseene yuhchihatestyetla, kwete kichi sooghatayehch, kiyinkhanata yakachi otsi ehtoole taoontya kiyehti.
MAR 8:12 Kahchu ohtye hoontatachehlon matsinde otsi, toowe ehtie, Yehoo gha ine chi hlioolate kiyinkhanata ehtoole, kwa hli anaghaisii, atuli ehtoole naghachualassi tine maghestyekne.

MAR 8:20 Kahchu taiuchi aline kootghasitich tikooh keoneti keoneti ketyi enetyi, tanetyi kloke ghaisis tadesmanooh nitalala nachatanatyetli? Toowe yehti, Taiuchi.

MAR 8:22 Kahchu yuhchityesha Bethsaida tsi; kahchu kiyananihtyi teze mataituehi, kahchu kichiniooghanunisit yakatutihi.
MAR 8:23 Kwa yila inchut ayi teze mataituehi, kahchu nito kwa tsi achuyetehti; kahchu yatai keghizek tsi, kahchu yakahtehtichoo, taoontya yehti ooli ghiila.
MAR 8:24 Kwa yachi tahyatii, kahchu ehti, Tane ghussi tachin etotyeni.
MAR 8:25 Kahchu yatai katehtich, kahchu yitige ookanahta yehti: kootoo oochu oontyei, kahchu aitye tane oochu ya-iine.
MAR 8:26 Kahchu yakwa tsi yeteha, toowe yehti ihe, Atu otsitaooya nito kwa, atuline chu tataoonti nito kwa.
MAR 8:27 Jesus hadesha, yaotatichne chu nito kwa tsi Caesarea Philippi; otsighatelu, Taootya yaotatichne tsi ehtilon kooh yehtiihe, Mea ali saghatya tane?

MAR 8:29 Kahchu toowe yehti, Ahwole mea ne ahlaoontye? Kahchu Peter ateyatetla kahchu toowela yehti, Nuni ayi Christ.
MAR 8:30 Kwa yatati atuli tsi hohohoontye atuneoo.
MAR 8:31 Kahchu tyi yaotatich, teze chue atghazi ooli kawotoohunne, kahchu yenaooli hoola ale, taneghaotatichi metihi chok yu, adesklesne chu, kahchu tsazughelyu, taiti dzinee kaohtzut ate kahchu tsitike naootezi.
MAR 8:32 Kahchu ehtii ayi zake tanenichi. Kahchu Peter nayanaun, kahchu tyi chuhootatyech.
MAR 8:33 Ahwole natanyesha tsi yaotatichne chu kanehtgha, Peter chuatatichlon, toowe ehti ihe, Satuchi otsidiya nuni atsinde metselihi: atu indi natih Nagha Tgha tsi tatyesuttih, yaghi zon ooli tane tsi tatyesuttih.
MAR 8:34 Kootoo tane tsi tyechi ehti yaotatichne chu toowe yehti, Ooline sakehtayeh ati, inkai oochit atu tuti ninayatooalli matsookhatayaghi, kahchu nakehtuyesi.
MAR 8:35 Ayih sye nayaghoohtalon kyehghata kehe, tghayatualassi; ayi tayadesooatai kyehghata kehe suni tsi kahchu otich oochu chu, ayi nayahoohtezi.
MAR 8:36 Yechiha yatsi ewoyuesiha tane, tike okehe ootanatsut ateoo, tatsinde tghayaghatyeshai ate?
MAR 8:37 Ooli sitah tyesooite taghatai tah?
MAR 8:38 Ooline siooyale tane zake chu chode soochaghachatai chu kyidyetseli chu tanegha; ayilon atane tane chue yaooyaooli, kootoo yuhchininiye ate kochi soochunetyi Matgha keotichne oochune chu.
MAR 9:1 KAHCHU toowe yehti, Kwa hli anaghaisii tahkine chode natehyane atu koozetse nio-olyezi, naooghatyitsi ooli ate Nagha Tgha kyeyi khoonao-ozit ate mehnatsutsutti yu.
MAR 9:2 Edzetaiti dzine kloe Jesus Peter chu, James chu, John chu hlyekeghatechlon, kwa hlilon otsighatyesha khis tsi ohtye nataghisatti sahte ooghatyi: kahchu yakaitghaityeh achu meotati.
MAR 9:3 Kahchu makestue mesaniti takulli kye yas etye; atuline chode tike hahtye takulli awolene ooli.
MAR 9:4 Kahchu klohnaaghityetl Elias chu Moses chu: kahchu inchiooghatatyech Jesus chu.
MAR 9:5 Kahchu Peter otyesta, toowehchu yehti Jesus tsi, Metihi, chode itli gha oochu: inkai taodatyedi kwa aootlihi; ehlaiti nuni gha, ehlaiti Moses gha, ehlaiti Elias gha.
MAR 9:6 Atu ti kehe atu ataotihi ihe; ohtye oonighanintyetl ihe.
MAR 9:7 Koos inchi ghililon: kahchu sa ooghatitsuklon yatsi, toowe otieh, Tai la se Chue menusli: Soozehtso.
MAR 9:8 Intyizon, aoonetye ookaghanehtha, atuline otehchi kiya-i, Jesus zon kooyehkatech.

MAR 9:10 Ayi zake kaghaghatalon, ayi yea tane zetse achatya oontye toon ghlaghatati.
MAR 9:11 Kahchu yehoo gha kiyehti, atesklesne yehoo gha koohatya oontya Elias hatse naoojezi?
MAR 9:12 Kahchu ateyatetlalon, yatatio yu, Kwa hli Elias hatse niya, atghe chu ooli sookah nioonila; kahchu takehnichatinkles akehe teze Chue ihe, natlon ooli kaootoohanu, kahchu menachule.
MAR 9:13 Ahwole suni anaghaisii aoontye, Elias kwa hli nooja, akooghati sikooh kooyekechatihtihch, takehnichatinkles akehe atanewoh.
MAR 9:14 Kahchu yaotatichne wohniya tsi, tane natlone yehkyatich chu yailon, adesklesne chu, taoontya tookooyetilon.
MAR 9:15 Kwete atghe kiya-ichi, atghe kaooghalilon, kahchu kichiodehsut yilaghinchutlon.
MAR 9:16 Kahchu adesklesne tsi, Taoontya nayeti?

MAR 9:19 Ateyatetlalon ahwole toowe ehti ihe, Naghai naghaje toontooe assine, taisa tsi otsi naghai aoostya aoontye, taisa tsi otsia naghatagha ooslya aoontye? Sananaha.
MAR 9:20 Kahchu kiyananiooh: ya-inchi kwete atsinde kooghoontye yayaghinchil; kahchu tike nadsut, kahchu yatatatyech maza tsi wohoos hailyi.

MAR 9:24 Kahchu kwete ayi tsito tatgha tsi otyesta, toowehchu ehti manatu ooli, Naghaghintai, kwa hwe a koosti; oochinootyeh atu a koodestihi.
MAR 9:25 Jesus wa-ichi tane yuhchiinhlaghatelyu, atsinde metselihi tsighotye, toowe yehtie, Nuni naza oontooehi najih oontooe ahoo yu atsinde nali, ohtye anesii, yuhchihadiya matsun tsi, atu chu mazi kahchu taooya.
MAR 9:26 Kahchu atsinde otyesta, kwete chu yayaghinchil, kahchu yatsun tsi ghotyesha: kahchu tane zetse etyelon; ayiwola natlone, Tehtsut ghatya aoontye.

MAR 9:35 Kachu nesta, keneti onketi matgha tyechi yehti, kahchu toowe yehti, Ooline haladi tooshai ate, ayi zon hli haistlachi ayuhezi, kahchu tane gha aoonetye gha keo-otechassi.

MAR 9:37 Mea so niyatuhtyeloo ehlaiti ayi etye tsito sindizi kehchi, suni nisatintyi: mea so nisatuhtyeloo, atu suni nisatintyi, ahwole ayi yuhchisatyehai.
MAR 9:38 Kahchu John ateyatetlalon, toowe ehtie, Meti, ghityii ehlaiti atsinde metselihi hayateha nindizi ihe, atu chu naghaketayeh: kahchu nanitun, atu naghaketayeh ihe.

MAR 9:40 Kahchu ayi atu chahte anahilai naghachinati.
MAR 9:41 Mea so chu naghaooalu ehlaiti tsa taismanni tu woton tsindizi ihe, Christ ezon manatunne aghli, kwa hli anaghaisii, atu tayatooalassi machitikechalihi.
MAR 9:42 Kahchu ooline chahteayile assine ehlaiti tidi natsutli aha sooti, otehchi ootaizi atunewoh mehnachahtune kazze tsye ma koos echaghesklu ate, tyechunnityeh ate tu chok ticha tsi.
MAR 9:43 Kahchu nila chahtontye ate, hlyenihul: otaihchi oochu nuni gha kweghoya tsaghatati achaghilihi ihe, nila onketi aghoo kochi otsitiya iyu aoontye khoon metselihi tsi, khoon atu nanatsaisti:
MAR 9:44 Nootya atu taikyehti, khoon chu atu nanatsaisti.
MAR 9:45 Kahchu nakye chahtontye ate, hlyenihul: otaihchi oochu nuni gha kweghoya tsaghatati achaghilihi ihe, nakye onketi aghoo ghotsinachatutyeissi khoon metselihi tsi, khoon atu nanatsaisti:
MAR 9:46 Noota atu taikyehti, khoon chu atu nanatsaisti.
MAR 9:47 Kahchu natai nihe atu oochu ookaitghai ate, hanehchul: otaihchi nawotaizi Nagha Tgha tike tsi oowooeissi natai ehlaiti ate, natai onketi aghoo ate otsinachatuhtyelassi khoon metselihi tsi:
MAR 9:48 Nootya atu taikyehti, khoon chu atu nanatsaisti.

MAR 9:50 Sootakun oochu: ahwole sootakun natuetah sootakun matghachilihi, tyeha kyehea sootakun matgha awahhliha? Sootakun kyatihch, kahchu oochu eotine klahtyi.

MAR 10:3 Kahchu ateyatetlalon toowehchu yehtilon Tyeha kehe anaghehtya Moses?
MAR 10:4 Kahchu toowe ghati, Moses hatyeh kha syeklah takehnichatinklessi o-ochatihtyehi adusklesse, kahchu oonchatoohtyellu.

MAR 10:11 Kahchu toowe yehti, Ooline assine tatsiyue odehtyetine achune chu makahtueh akoochaile ate, soocha keoach.
MAR 10:12 Tsege chu tatsiyue odehtye, kahchu makahtueh achilai ate, soocha keoach.

MAR 10:16 Kahchu yakoone ihe niyatintyih, kahchu yakatihtich, kahchu oochu yachi indityesa.
MAR 10:17 Kahchu yitai tyesha atghunni kehchi, ehlaityi yuhchidesha ghatlelhu, kahchu yachinachustatalih, kahchu taoontya yehti toowe, Oochu Tanechiotyihi, tawoshlaitea ahtaze wostazonha?

MAR 10:21 Kahchu Jesus yakaihtgha, yuntyeh, kahchu toowe yehti, Ehlaiti ooli nainchi tehtsut: ootinta, anehisi anetye mataghaneli, tyetaisanaine chu ghatanale, kooh ohtye natatootlosi ya tehchi: kahchu yuhchidiya, kahchu ninaootiah ayi sookatiya-i, kahchu sakehtiya.
MAR 10:22 Kahchu atu oochu indityesalon ayi sa tachi, kahchu yitaityeshalon atu keneli ihe: atane ohtye ooli natlon ayehilon.
MAR 10:23 Kahchu Jesus klokanatgha, toowehchu yehti yaotatichne, Tewotye atatlate ayine matatintlone kwewootyelassi Nagha Tgha nataghe tsi!
MAR 10:24 Kahchu yaotatichne yazake kaooghulli. Ahwole Jesus ateyatetla kahchu toowehchu yehti, Tsitohkaine, tahtye indatlaooh ayi kiyasooli mehmehetatintlon kwewootyelu Nagha Tgha mataghe tsi!

MAR 10:26 Kahchu o-ochi ghaooghali, toowe hlaghatati ihe, Mea mechua aghatoota?
MAR 10:27 Kahchu Jesus yakaitgha toowe yehti, Tane la atu kookechi nioontsit, Nagha Tgha la atu kooh hoontye: aha atane Nagha Tgha atghezi ooli mahechi nioontsut.
MAR 10:28 Kahchu Peter toowe tyi ehti, Nea anetye kyecho tasityetl, kahchu naghakyehtasityetliih.
MAR 10:29 Kahchu Jesus ateyatetla toowehchu yehti, Kwa hli anaghaisii, atuline ooli kyeghachelihine nito kwa, mekye koole, mehtghizze koole, matgha koole, ma koole, matsiyue koole, maskyege koole, matsatle koole, suni ihe, kahchu otich oochu ihe,
MAR 10:30 Ahwole otsinitooalassi keonetyi ketyi otehche enetyi too, nito kwane, mekye chu, mehtghizze chu, matgha chu, ma chu, maskyege chu, makyeyih chu, kahchu tsookatayaghi chu; kahchu achu tike tsi ghonioonizut ate kwihzon ehchaghatai.

MAR 10:35 Kahchu James chu John chu, Zebidee chue, yachidesha toowe yehtie, Taneghaotatyechi, kooh koosti aghaawoti akootiti sikooh.
MAR 10:36 Kahchu toowe yehti, Yea sachi nioonunassit naghachanasti?
MAR 10:37 Kahchu toowe kiyehti, Aghaniah sookah ookyetih, ehlaiti mehoonati, hligi oosklaghazihtye, nachatinchai kehe.
MAR 10:38 Ahwole Jesus toowe yehtii, Atu atawahti ooli ootahkyeti: atu mihe tuwahto la ayi tsa mehtughastoii? kahchu tunaghakechuhkliti ayi mehtanekhaitsikliti ayi ihe nehsakaichihkliti?
MAR 10:39 Toowe kiyehti, Atu ahandatla. Kahchu Jesus toowe yehti, Kwa hli tuwahtoissi tsaeh ieti otsi tughastode; kahchu ayi mehtukhaitsihkliti mihe tusakechihklitii ihe ayi ihe mihe naghakyechuhklitassi:
MAR 10:40 Ahwole wostyi chisinchi chu tayaghetichu oosklaghazintye atu si senatunne eli kaooali; ahwole kooyachulelassi ayine kooya so-ochesunne,
MAR 10:41 Kahchu kenetyine aghatitsuk chi ohtye atu yatsi kyehanelilon James chu John chu.

MAR 14:2 Ahwole toowe ghati, Atu kwehlachayihi dzinee eli, atu tane inchiniootyelu.
MAR 14:3 Kahchu ali Bethany tih ma kwa oota Simon chusache ayahi, intyizon sata atsitsoo yuhchidesha tsege ayahi khluk tsye khaisis spikenard otsi o-ochi matahi natlon; kwa yataghataghiooh tsye khaisis yitii, kahchu yatsi kachi nayadinklit.
MAR 14:4 Kahchu tahkine atu keghaneli kooyindi ihe, kahchu toowe ghati, Yehoo gha tidi ghatsatyestka tidi khluk?
MAR 14:5 Ayi ihe ataghachulekhai ootaihchi tghakooh keoneti ketyi sooniya satsoone ihe, kahchu tyetaisanaini ghachulelakhai Kwa atu tyeswa akiyehti.
MAR 14:6 Kahchu Jesus toowe yehti, Atu machuwahti; yehoo gha machisootahtyihch? ootaihchi oochu saatyiinh.
MAR 14:7 Ayi ihe inkaa eahline tyita-isanaini, kahchueoonetye akootahti o-ochi akooyaghaklehesi: ahwole suni atu inkaa naghaasli.
MAR 14:8 Koo hatiih machitelonh otsi: ootagha yuhchidesha yuhthlukke gha oochu nisachutyel gha.
MAR 14:9 Kwa hli anaghaisii, ooli tsi otsi tidi otich oochu moohwochatih ate tike okehe, tidi chu atii moohwochutyehesi menachutihi gha.
MAR 14:10 Kwa Judas Iscariot, ehlaiti keneti onketi matgha ihe, yinkhadesha taneghaotatyech tyiyi metihe, ooli oochadehyitli kyehodewoosh,
MAR 14:11 Oohaditsuk chi, ohtye kyehanelilon, kahchu ootagha tsi akiyehtilon sooniya yaooleli. Oonkhanatghalon tyehakehe sookah nio-otsitigha chahte autigha.
MAR 14:12 Kahchu hatseto dzinee otles atu chihyutli, kwa ooghazehai passover, yaotatichne toowe yehti, Tyecha koodinti nataghasootaoozani ate oonsitsi passover?
MAR 14:13 Kwa onkye tane yaotatichne yitai tyeha, toowehchu yehti, otsitahtyel nitc kwa otyiyi tsi, kwa matiti tagahazasi ehlaiti teze yaalu ehlaiti mehtukachatayehi taismanooh tu ihe: makehtahaz.
MAR 14:14 Kahchu kwiyesitti, toowe ahti nito kwa kakaoodzutti, Wondi achune kwa ayiti o-ositsti passover mawotastihchne chu?
MAR 14:15 Kahchu wahih naghotyesi ooncha tyinta ooli chelohchi soowachisunooh: Naghani gha iehti soowatahsun.
MAR 14:16 Kahchu yaotatichne yitaighatestyetl yuhchi chu ghatatyetl nito kwa otyiyi tah, kahchu woghiooh akooyehtiii: kahchu yatagha sooghihsun passover.
MAR 14:17 Kahchu kahkachi yuhchinooja keneti onketi matgha chu.
MAR 14:18 Ghadehtsi zahtgha kahchu eghinta, Jesus toowe ehti, Kwa hli anaghaisii, ehlaiti naghai chodeh sakaehtane chahte asooti.
MAR 14:19 Kwa tyi atu keghaneli, toowe chu tyi ghati ehlaityekooh, Suni la? kahchu hligi toowe ehti, Suni la?
MAR 14:20 Kwa ateyatetla toowehchu yehti, Ehlaiti kenetye onketi matgha ihe, yaghi tila ihe setyi tsa ihkye.
MAR 14:21 Tane Chue otsidesha, takehnichatinkles akehe otye: ahwole atu oochu oontye yaghi teze tane Chue mihe atahachahi! otaihchi o-otaizilon yaghi teze ihe atu niniya tah ooli ate.
MAR 14:22 Kahchu aghatsitsoo zahtgha, Jesus otles tyes nidiooh, kahchu merci ehti, kahchu tahke yataghiyits, kahchu yayiooh, toowehchu yehti, Nitahah, mehtah: tila sazi alonte.
MAR 14:23 Kahchu nidiooh mehtusato, kwa kayioo merci, yayiooh: kwa aghanetye ayite otsi tughatolon.
MAR 14:24 Ahwole toowe yehtiii, Tila sa tulle alontye aduskles koonde ihe, oontlo chi otsi tyechihkliti.
MAR 14:25 Kwa hli anaghaisii, atu otaihchi wostoissi machiche chiche tu chine tsi, kwa hlilon yaghi dzinee kwa kootyinusto Nagha Tgha nataghe.
MAR 14:26 Ehlaiti hyin eghachinooh khis Olives tsighatestyetl.
MAR 14:27 Kahchu Jesus toowe yehti, Anahtye naghai atu oochu inditaghaaisi suni ihe too hatleke: takenichatinkles akehe, Ooshalasi aspai ghaghatai haslai ite eonetye tsi natihassi aspai.
MAR 14:28 Ahwole tsitike netai ate, naghaghatuhshezi Galilee tsi.
MAR 14:29 Ahwole Peter toowe ehti, Inkai ahwole aghanetye atu keyaneli ate, hoontye koole suni intue.
MAR 14:30 Kahchu Jesus toowe ehti, Kwa hli anesi, tidi dzinee, tidi hatleke te, atu ka onkye otyesta tyeh tihchok, tghakooh oohchit soota-ghoontyesi.
MAR 14:31 Ahwole otaihchi otatich hloduhsi kootieh, Ahwole nakah tanesut ate ooh, atu ihlawotatehoo oohchit noodustyeisi. Kwakooye otye aghatane chu ghatiih.
MAR 14:32 Kwa koohnaghintyetl Gethsemane woyetih: kahchu toowe yehti yaotatichne, Nea aghasushlito sakyeh.
MAR 14:33 Kahchu yehtestyetl Peter chu James chu John chu, kahchu o-ochi inditatunne, kahchu matsun tah oonkhail acha;
MAR 14:34 Kahchu toowe yehti, Setsinde o-ochi atu oochu atyetatih azetse tsi: chode ahthli naghai, kachchu ootanahi.
MAR 14:35 Oontsutle wotaihai otsidesha-ih, kahchu tike wozohtih nwatsutooh kooyekehchi tyetaooklihassi sye makehchi nio-otsilon, ayi sakehchi ahalai yao-otsitooh.
MAR 14:36 Kahchu toowe ehti, Abba, Tyeih, atghazi ooli tahnootsut nuni; tidi mehtutsato saoone naniah: hoontye ooli atu suni koosti, ahwole nuni koodinti.
MAR 14:37 Kwa yahchidesha, nayintyi satyi, kwa toowe yehti Peter tsi, Simon sintyi la? atu ooniila ehlaiti meoochanehoo?
MAR 14:38 Oonahi kahchu aghasuhli, atu otsitawohazu inkaoochatauntih. Atsinde kwa hili ootagha, ahwole khazi chintyeh.
MAR 14:39 Kwa yitai tyesha, kahchu oghasulih, kahchu kwaka ayi zake ihe ootatich.
MAR 14:40 Kwa oondehsha tsi, yintyi satyi kahchu, (kootai ya-inkhai ihe,) kahchu atu ataooati atyekitooklih akehe.
MAR 14:41 Kwa tghakooh yuhchidesha, kahchu toowe yehti, Kwa hwe satyih, kahchu niwotahah mehnatahyihi: kwakoole, meoochanehoo koohnaooizut; nea, tezoo Chue mataghachila taghila tsi metselineh.
MAR 14:42 Niahtyel, yitai tawotyeli; nea, yaghi atu oochu asehti kwa hwa atih.
MAR 14:43 Kwa kwete, kwaka ootayehchoo, yuhchidesha Judas, ehlaiti keneti onketi matgha ihe, kahchu yihtestyetlooh tane natlone simakanis pyese chu mekachatayitli chu, taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyi ihe, adesklesne chu hoola atityine ihe.
MAR 14:44 Ooh yaghi atu oochu ayehtii yayiooiih mehyachanitsiyi, toowe ehtieh, Ootayatasihchassine wohtsiewohzyesi, ayila ayi; nintahtyeh, kahchu yitai tahtyeh tyekeh.
MAR 14:45 Itetyeh nooja ate, tsiwohzon yatsi anaatahlu, toowehchu ehti, Metihi, metihi; kwa yunchighachi.
MAR 14:46 Kwa kiyinchut, kahchu nighadintyi.
MAR 14:47 Ooh hligi ayiti naghatehyane tapyese natyese ghayaghachut, kahchu nayatehul yakeotihchi taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyi, kahchu yatsakhe tyeghihyitl.
MAR 14:48 Kwa Jesus ateyatetla, toowehchu yehti, Ihla unoosi khataachila pyese intyesi yu mekachatayitli yu tala nisataghahtyelu?
MAR 14:49 Naghaikyastaichii dzinee eonetye tyetachatlihi kwa oota taneghaodintihchoo, atu chu nisatahtyiih: ahwole aduskles tyiyi tikewokyeli.
MAR 14:50 Kahchu aghanetye kiyechodestyetl, kahchu taghoghadehsut.
MAR 14:51 Kwa ehlaiti uskye yakehtehtla, atetakhatooh chatachi takulli intyedi ati; kwa uskyegoone kiyinchut:
MAR 14:52 Kwa kyechodyesha ayi chatachi takulli, kahchu tghihklah intyedi.
MAR 14:53 Kwa Jesus otsighatehtyi o-ochi taneghaotatihchi metihi tyiyi atindi: kwa ayiti atghe yeghati taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyi hoolaatitine chu, adusklesne chu.
MAR 14:54 Kahchu Peter tontyetsi otsi yakehtehklalon, koole taneghaotatyehchi metihi kwa tsi: kwa yakasatah keotichne, na-itziluh khoon tsi.
MAR 14:55 Kahchu taneghaotatihchne metihi tyiyi atghe chu tsiwatilhne oohatakyet otsi ayi ihe atu oochu akiyuti Jesus kizuheli; ahwole atu oontsutli ohwiooh.
MAR 14:56 Ayi ihe natlone ketseli hakehe athlaghatati, ahwole atu hleoontye taghasoocha ootihe.
MAR 14:57 Ooh tahkine nighintyetl, kahchu atu tyeswe akiyehti, toowe ghati ihe,
MAR 14:58 Tasitsukiih atyakehe, Chahtehaooslyeasi tidi tyetachatlihi kwa tyiyi atsila kila ihe, kahchu taiti dzinee aoocha ate achu nao-osiasi atu kila ihe.
MAR 14:59 Ahwole aghatane chu koosoochawotihi atu hleoontye.
MAR 14:60 Kwa o-ochi taneghaotatyechi metihi tyiyi ookaze nazut, Jesus tsi taoontya yehti toowe ehti ihe, Atu atyetunatla la? ye ihea ayi chahte anaghatya oontye?
MAR 14:61 Ahwole atu wotih, atula kehe chu atih. Kahchu o-ochi taneghaotayehchi metihi tyiyi taoontya yehti, kahchu toowe yehti, Nuni la ayi Christ, ma, Chue Moohchuootaya?
MAR 14:62 Kwa Jesus toowe ehti, Suni ayi: kahchu wahyesi tezoo Chue satah noohtichintye mehnatsutsutti, kahchu yahchityesha khoos yataihchi.
MAR 14:63 Kahchu taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyi tanatunne yaghinchil, kahchu toowe ehti, Ye ootaihchi a soochawochatihi khasootiti aoontye?
MAR 14:64 Ootahtsukiih mehe atachochihliti: naghani ta kootahtya? Kwa aghanetye kooye hlaghatati tsazuheli gha.
MAR 14:65 Kwa tahkine tyekookeghizek, kahchu yani aghataghighat, kahchu nakiteghul, kahchu toowe kiyehti ihe, atanataihchi woka atawonati: Kwa keotihchne nakiteghul taghila tyiyi ihe.
MAR 14:66 Kwa Peter yiyue nito kwa otyiyi ti ati, yuniya ehlaiti taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyi yawoai tsege:
MAR 14:67 Kwa ya-i tsi Peter naitziluh, yakaitghah, toowehchu ehti, Kahchu nuni chu mehtintyetliih Jesus Nazareth otsi tatyezutti.
MAR 14:68 Ahwole atu a kooti, toowe ehti ihe, Atu atawosti, kahchu atu atasuk atintya kehe. Kwa hadesha tyinta weswatih otsi; kwa tihchok tayidze otyesta.
MAR 14:69 Kwa keotihche tsege kahchu ya-ih, kahchu toowe tyiyehti ayine ieti nehyane, Taila ehlaiti ayine tsi.
MAR 14:70 Kwa kahchu atu koo ooli ehtilon. Kahchu kluhtai ayine ayite nazati, toowehchu ghati Peter, Nuni nooklya ehlaiti aghatane tsi, ayi ihe Galilaean nali, kahchu kooh nakasse atih.
MAR 14:71 Ahwole ketseliooh wotyihi zon ohtye ootatyehch, toowe akehe, Atu atesti tai teze mamawotahtyehchi.
MAR 14:72 Kwa onkye tihchok tayidze otyesta. Kwa Peter kyechetehti yaghi sa Jesus ayehtiii, Atu ka ootihoo tihchok tayidze onkye tghakooh oochit sootahoontyezi. Kwa hlilon ayiti indi ootinta, nachaihchahli.
MAR 15:1 Kwa kwete hatlindo aooclia taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyino aglianctye ehlakehe tsiwohzon tsiwowatilh hoolya atitine chu adeskilesne chu hlito aonetyo gha tsiwoghatsihi ihe ayi chu, kwa Jesus tahkiyagheskluli, kahchu achukcdehtyi, kwa kikalmihtyj, Pilate.
MAR 15:2 Kahchu Pilato taoontya yehti, Nunila o-ochi koometihi tyiyi nalila Jewne gha? Kahchu ateyatetla toowohcliu yehti, Kooh tintya oontye.
MAR 15:3 Kahchu taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyine chahte akiyehti ooU nation ihe: ahwole atulakehe atyeta-i.
MAR 15:4 Kwa Pilate kahchu taoontya yehti, toowe ehtie, Atu lakehe atyetanatla la? nea taitya ooli ihe chahte anaghahti.
MAR 15:5 Ahwole Jesus zon atu la kehe aye ih kwaka; kwa Pilate kawoolih,
MAR 15:6 Ooh ayi kwelachayihi ehlaiti yezookeh ninkho kvyechehtyesne, ooli assine nioonunisit assine.
MAR 15:7 Ayiti hligi aliih Barabbas ooyeih, ihkahtah chaghcsklu ayi yekatichii kwa akiyila indiootaunne, kootoo tane zehai ihe indiootatunne ayiti.
MAR 15:8 Kwa natlone ohtye oghatyesta tyi yatsi niooghanunisit kooh hakiyulihi gha kooli zon hakooyilai ihe.
MAR 15:9 Ahwole Pilate ateyatetla, toowc ehti ihe, Kooh kootahti la naghachitulisitisi Jewne o-ochi koometihi tyiyi?
MAR 15:10 Atawahtihi ihe taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyine yatagliintsutti ihe mihe incho-otsa-i.
MAR 15:11 Ahwole taneghaotatyehchi metihi tyiyine kichinagbayyh tanene, otaihai koochitootsittih Barabbas.
MAR 15:12 Kooh kahehu Pilate ateyatetla toowehchu yehti, Takootahtya oslihasi kooh yachi o-ochi koometihi tyiyi Jewnu woyi?
MAR 15:13 Kahchu toowe hakehe oghatesta, Klaniaitih ninihtyeh.
MAR 15:14 Kahchu Pilate toowe ehti, Ye tahtye ketselihoo atyuntye? Kahchu ootailichi o-ochi oghatesta, Klaniaitih ninihtyeh.
MAR 15:15 Kwa Pilate tane gha kasootati chatupisha gha, yakahninooh Barabbas assi, kahchu Jesus taghinchut, yakhataghitoos, klaniai nichuhtyelu,
MAR 15:16 Kwa simakanisne otsighatelityi tsiwatyehli kwa, Praetorium wotsatiti; kwa tyechi kiyehti ihlakehe atghe simakanisne.
MAR 15:17 Kwa natunne taghaghintyi aghapai tatulli ihe, kahchu kiyesklu o-ochi metihi tyiyi chahte hoos chok chine ihe, kwa ita tsi gha ghiooh,
MAR 15:18 Kahchu tyi yila ooghatthoon toowe, Yea mehsuchanetyi, o-ochi kookaoonazut Jewne.
MAR 15:19 Kahchu nakidehul yetsi chokhaiye chok ihe, kahchu kikighizek, kwa yatsitaghatsunne ootooghanehzil yatsi nachusghataklihl
MAR 15:20 Kahchu kiyachatyetl atghe tatulli nighadinchus, kwa yanatunne taghintyetl, kahchu ghooghatehtyi klaniai tih.
MAR 15:21 Kwa kitahnaghutsut ehlaiti Simon Cyrenian ali, ayi kwo noojai, tane natihti otsi Jiadesha, kootgha Alexander chu Rufus chu tahchiyatyilyu klaniai.
MAR 15:22 Kwa nakiyintyi Golgotha tsi, ayi tsati, Atsitsunne ooli te.
MAR 15:23 Kwa kiyayiooh tyewootoo chiche chok tu mathuchunihklito myrrh: ahwole atu niyadiooh.
MAR 15:24 Kwa klaniai nikiyinti tsi yanatunne atatghahaistla kikaootehunni ihe, ye anetye teze nitioolola.
MAR 15:25 Kahchu oolitah tghakooh mehooachanehoo awatazoo dzinee ooli klaniadi nikiyinti tsi.
MAR 15:26 Kwa achatyesklesi ayiti otsi chahteachatei takehnichanikuchiih ookeh. O-OCHI KOO METIHI TYIYI JEWNE.
MAR 15:27 Kahchu onkyetine chu klaniadi nighanihtye unoosine; ehlaiti noohtechintye, ooh hligi chu oosklaghazihtye.
MAR 15:28 Achudesklessi tyiyi tikenikyih, kooh woti, Kahcliu metseline kah kachanehtyeh.
MAR 15:29 Ooh ayine kweghatilhne ayiti oontlochi kiye sootatyihch, tagliatsi ghaghado, toowehchu ghati ihe, Gha, nuni chahta wonatla tyctachatlihi kwa tyiyi, kahchu aoonatla taiti dzinee,
MAR 15:30 Aghatinta, kahchu yulichinootinta klauiai tsi.
MAR 15:31 Aghatane chu o-ochi taneghaotatyech metihi tyiyine ontlonchi akiyehti ihe toowe hlaghatati ihe adesklesne chu, Nayaghehta achune chu; atai koochati atu aghatatah.
MAR 15:32 Oonka Christ o-ochi koometihi tyiyi Israel kwa hoote klaniai tsi noodoojai, wowoti khai kahchu a kootahti. Oo ayi yakah klaniadi yakah atii yataUhlon.

MAR 15:34 Kwa kalaketye sa kehchi ahalai klodesha, Jesus otyesta takaseneodi, toowe ehti ihe, Eloi, eloi, lama sabachthani? ayi tsati ihe Saghintai, Saghintai, yehoo gha sechanila aoontye.
MAR 15:35 Ooh tahkine ieti nehyane, ooghatitsuk tsi, toowe ghati, Nea, Ehas kawotih.
MAR 15:36 Kahchu ehlaiti otsidehta, kahchu tsifo yeatatih atihih tayatohman sootakan chiche tu ihe, kahchu nayiooh klokhayetgha, kahchu yayiooh tuwoto gha, toowe ehti ihe, Atu kinditaghaha; oonka oohwoti Elias nayaghintyi kha.
MAR 15:37 Kahchu Jesus otyesta ohtye ati, kahchu matsinde kwa yecheli.
MAR 15:38 Kwa ayi hlichatinkatii tyetachatlihi kwa otyiyi intyizon tghahkyehchi wotye cheghinchil yataihchi ctis yiyue otsi.
MAR 15:39 Kwa kootoo ayi simakunis metihi, ooghade nazutih. wa-i kooakehe otyesta, kahchu yatsinde yechelihooh, toowe ehti, tyewotye tune teze Nagha Tgha Chue ghilelon.

MAR 15:41 (Aghatane chu, Galilee atichi, kikehtyeshaii, kahchu ooli kooyaalih ine;) achune chu natlone tsegoo yuhchi kooyehkyatehch ine Jerusalem tsi.
MAR 15:42 Kahkachi aoocha tsi, ayi ihe intyizon dzmee tsi watsehlu ayi dzinee Manejitti dzinee tsi ooh,

MAR 16:1 Kwa Menejitti Dzinee kawohtzut tsi, Mary Magdalene, kahchu Mary James ma kahchu Salome, ataghaghila yu natunetgha tsehklutti, kwa yuhchighatatyel ate yu ihe kiyuhklukooh.
MAR 16:2 Kwri o-ochi hatse hatlindo ooli hatse dzinee, koohnioonizuiooh natine kwa otsighatestyetl sa niiya tsi.
MAR 16:3 Kwa toowe hlaghatati, Mea tsye yughaii aoontye natine kwa tustla otsi?
MAR 16:4 Kwa koo ookahanehtghachi, oogha-ih tsye achuhchi nichunihailo: o-ochi ghinchai ihe.
MAR 16:5 Kwa kwegliaghintyetlooh native kwa oota, kiya-ih ehlaiti uskye noohtichintye chisinchi sata, tasata natyesooh makestue takulooh: kwa oonighanintyetl.
MAR 16:6 Kwa toowe yehti, Atu oonighahtyel: Minkhanatgha Jesus Nazareth otsi tatyezutti, klaniai nichanihtyiii: tsitike ghinta-ih; atu chode ali; nea nighamhtyintiii.
MAR 16:7 Ahwole ootahtab, yaotatichne tatahtih Peter chu naghaghadesha Galilee tsi: ayiti wahiiissi, naghatatyakehe.
MAR 16:8 Kwa kwete khooghatastyetl, kahelm natine kwa otsi tsmtestyetl; yaghatilonlon ihe kahchu khaooghali ihe: kahchu atuline atula kehe akiyeliti; oonighanintyetlon ihe.
MAR 16:9 Kootoo Jesus niiyaii kwete hatse dzine tsiwatselhu, hatse yuhniya Mary Magdalene, ayi taiuchi atsmde metselihi yazi otsi hayateha-ii.
MAR 16:10 Kwa yatatilon ayine yehkyatehchiine, tsukye atu yaghita zahtgha kahchu ghutsakooh
MAR 16:11 Ooh ahatane, ooghatitsuk tsi ghata, kahchu ya-ilon atu a kooghati.
MAR 16:12 Ayi keh yuhniya achu akelie ouketye agha tane Ihe, ghataihlu zahtgha, achu tike tsi otsighataihlu.
MAR 16:13 Kwa kitati achune: kahchu ama kiyehti ayine.
MAR 16:14 Atyechi yuhniya keneti ehlaiti matgha aline ghiskye eghehta ootye, kwa yachiotatyech majih oontooei kha kahchu yadzye natsutti kha atu a kiyuti ihe ayi yakehtghaii tsitike nesta.

MAR 16:17 Kahchu tidi yachanahai yakeh ootoohsitassi ayine a kootine; Sindizi ihe kehchi ghakitoolelassi atsinde metselihi; oogliatootyehchassi atsoodi koondi ihe;
MAR 16:18 Nootuze nighatoohtyelassi; oolita tughato ate chu azetse ahi, atu kootoohtihassi; kikatootiassi yatitihne, kwa oochu nakiyaooliteissi.
MAR 16:19 Kwa Naghaghatai sakyedesutooh, yatai otsi nichatihtyi, kahchu sata-ih Nagha Tgha noohtichintye.
MAR 16:20 Ahwole achughatestyetl, tike okehe tane chiwoghatihi ihe, Naghaghatai kahkeooghatihchoo, kahchu sa natsut oola kaootsooli chu intghaninkhi. Amen.
